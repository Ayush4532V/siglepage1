ABOUT US

Welcome to a world where we don't just build supercars — we create experiences that ignite every sense. Founded by Shivank Verma & Nishant Mitthal, our brand has quickly won the hearts of automotive enthusiasts across Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Punjab, Telangana, and is accelerating into new territories every day. We deliver uncompromising performance, design, and craftsmanship, pushing the boundaries of what's possible on four wheels. Each vehicle is a symbol of power, precision, and purpose — from the fierce growl of the engine to the elegance of every curve and contour. Whether it's the raw intensity of a track-ready beast or the refined luxury of a grand tourer, our supercars are engineered for those who demand more than just speed — they demand soul. We don’t just build cars. We define motion.
